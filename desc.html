Visualization Insights study

Welcome. Thank you for choosing to participate in this study!

This study will consist of 90 trials (rounds) in total. The trials will be divided into three blocks (of 30 trials each). We will introduce the visualizations used and any changes to your task before each block. Please read these instructions carefully as it will change for every block of trial.

In each trial, we will show you 10 graphs, and you will need to make decisions based on these graphs. We estimate each trial to take approximately 30 seconds. So the entire study will take approximately 45 mins. If you want to quit, please click on the exit button that will appear on each trial page, and you will be compensated for the number of trials that you've completed.

Note: we do not save your progress, so please do not close this tab once you begin the study!
